WHY ARE DOGS THE BEST PETS?

Dogs rule! Although there is a greater overall population of cats in the United States, more household have dogs! They are truly a man's best friends, and they will be loyal companions to your family!

Cats, on the other hand, are sassy and scratchy. While they can be cute and furry on the outside, they are only full of fury on the inside. They are natural-born trouble makers! Although dogs aren't perfect, they have hearts of gold.
